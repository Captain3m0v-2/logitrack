Troubleshooting

Why can't I see my data?

Make sure your data source is properly connected and that you have the correct permissions set up.

How do I reset my password?

Go to Settings > Security and use the password reset option.

Why are my charts not updating?

Check your data refresh settings and ensure your internet connection is stable.
